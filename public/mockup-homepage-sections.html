How it works

Borrow, lend, or pass it on — in four moves.

01
Step 01

List what you've got

Post gear you're not using. Set your own terms — lend it, gift it, or offer it to followers only.

02
Step 02

Browse & request

Search by category, camp, or keyword. Send a borrow request — the owner confirms the handoff.

03
Step 03

Coordinate pickup

Agree on where and when. All logistics live in your inventory dashboard — no side-channel needed.

04
Step 04

Return it (or don't)

Mark the return when it's done. Or list it again for the next person. Keep the cycle going.

Why it matters
I

Radical Interdependence

Others may have what you need. You may have what others need. Turns out that's enough.

II

Decommodification, Actually

Make it a year-round thing. Every shared item is one less Amazon order. Do the math.

III

Lending is a Type of Gifting

You don't even have to give it away. Let someone borrow it. That counts.

IV

Not Going This Year?

Your gear still can. Let it earn its keep while it collects dust in your garage.

The playa provides — because you do.

What this site actually does, in plain English.

I
For lenders

Your gear earns its keep

List items you're not using. Set visibility, lending terms, and a return date. Your inventory, your rules.

II
For borrowers

Get what you need without buying it

Browse community gear. Request to borrow. Skip the Amazon order and the post-burn storage problem.

III
For your camp

Share within your circle

Restrict items to campmates or followers. Keep the sharing tight — or open it to the whole community.

IV
For the planet

One less thing in a landfill

Gear that gets shared gets used. Gear that gets used doesn't get thrown away. It's really that simple.
